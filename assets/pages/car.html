Driver Guide

Vehicle requirements


Does your vehicle qualify to drive with ? Most four-door cars do, but requirements vary by vehicle option.


#ForWeb

Minimum requirements


  • Yellow-plated vehicle (Commercial registration)
  • Model year 2012 or newer
  • 4-door hatchback, sedan, SUV, or auto
  • Good condition, no damage and environment friendly

Registration Certificate

We require a copy of your current registration with the information clearly visible. If the vehicle is in another's name, we also require the NOC/Affidavit


Insurance

We require a copy of your current insurance policy with all information clearly visible.


Permit

We require a copy of your current permit with all information clearly visible.


Driving licence

We require a copy of your current driving licence with all information clearly visible.


PAN card

We require a copy of your current PAN card with all information clearly visible.


Vehicle options in

Prime SUV

Most four doors SUV car qualify to drive with Prime SUV

Additional requirements

  • 4-door SUV in good condition
  • Seat at least 5 passengers in addition to the driver
  • Working windows and air conditioning
  • Good and hygiene interior

Cars model year 2012 or newer SUV qualify to drive with Prime SUV. eg: Innova crysta

Prime Sedan

Most four doors sedan car qualify to drive with Prime Sedan

Additional requirements

  • 4-door SUV in good condition
  • Seat at least 4 passengers in addition to the driver
  • Working windows and air conditioning
  • Good and hygiene interior

Cars model year 2012 or newer sedan qualify to drive with Prime sedan. eg: Indigo, Ciaz

Auto

Most auto qualify to drive with Auto

Additional requirements

  • Auto in good condition
  • Seat at least 2 passengers in addition to the driver
  • Good and hygiene interior

Auto model year 2012 or newer qualify to drive with Auto.
