FAQ

How will I know when I'm getting a ride request? How do I accept?

When you get a trip request, your screen will flash and a sound will ping. You have 30 seconds to accept the trip request. Just press accept on your screen to accept.

I didn't start or end a trip on-time. What do I do?

Go into your Trip History and tap on the trip. At the bottom of the screen, tap "Need Help" and then "I had an issue with this fare." You can raise issue "I didn't begin or end the trip on time" and fill in the pick-up and drop-off locations. We'll make the adjustment!

My rider wants me to wait. What should I do?

If a rider asks you to wait, you're welcome to do so. Just keep the trip running. However, if you don't want to or can't wait, just ask the rider to request another Uber when they're done.

What if the rider wants to change destinations or make multiple stops?

That's OK! Don't end the trip, just ask the rider to delete their previous destination in the rider app and enter the new address. You'll still be paid for any time and distance traveled.

When and how do I get paid?

are paid via direct deposit weekly for money earned. Payment is made by direct deposit only.

Do I need to register on Your site to book ride?

Yes,You need to download the app then you have to register yourself as user.You have to provide all details for registration.

What if the car doesnt show up?

In case the vehicle you doesnot show then can contact to us by customer support,we are there to help you.

What is RV cabs peak time charge?

RV cabs peak time charge is little more than usual charge,it is a fare-rationalizing rule of using a multiplier X.

Can i request a taxi to wait at a place?

We require the pick up address be a business address or a user known address.
